Q: How do I get started?

A: Navigate to the Assess Job page and fill out the form to include important details about the job post you’re interested in. Fields with stars are required. You have the option to enter your email if you want the job to be saved.

Q: How can I review my saved jobs?

A: Navigate to the Saved Jobs tab to view all jobs that have been saved.
