This app is built to help job-seekers assess the legitimacy of job posts they might be interested in. In the Assess Job page, you can input job post information and get a summary of potential risks and flagged elements. You will also have the option to save the job post by providing your email. Saved job posts can then be revisited in the Saved Jobs page. Since the saved posts will be public, please do not put any sensitive information into the form. Visit the Help page for more information on how to use this website.

Disclaimer: This app has its limitations. An element being flagged doesn't automatically mean that the job is fake or fraudulent. By the same token, this app may not flag everything that could be suspicious. Please exercise caution and use your best judgement when deciding whether to give out your personal information.
